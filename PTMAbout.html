About the Website

This project is made by PSHS-MC Grade 9 Computer Science curicullum as a project output. The topic proper time management was chosen from the common issue Pisay students suffer from- cramming. We eventually realized that cramming is just the effect of ineffective time management and as they say, the way to solve a problem is to solve its root. These reasons bore fruit this website.

And as for this site being filled with anime pictures, please bear in mind that we just love anime and think that anime pictures would suit the way we wrote the tips. BTW the girl at the background pic is gorgeous.
**totally biased opinion XD
