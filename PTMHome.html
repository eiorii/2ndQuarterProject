“Ok. I’m gonna focus on my reqs today. No phones, no distractions, just reqs.”
---Five minutes later---
“You know what? Reqs can go to hell for all I care. I’m just gonna sleep.”

Familiar scene to you? Well, I bet a lot of people experienced this moment at least once. There really are times when you don't feel like being productive and just want to take a break and procrastinate everything next time.
But doing this wastes time. Time that you could've spent on other things like advance reading or playing your favorite game. So what can you do to save your time? Well, (I know that you already know this based on the title but pretend that you don't hahaha) you can improve your time-management skills. But what is time management?

Time Management

Time management is the effective planning and distribution of time alloted for each task of the day. Practicing proper time-management is highly necessary for Pisay students because it will help you be very, very productive (oohh productive? Sounds foreign hahaha). This will also help you have more leisure time and to be honest who doesn't want more leisure time, right?
