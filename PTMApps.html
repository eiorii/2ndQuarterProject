timers!

to prevent overworking or losing track of time while on breaks, using timers is a good solution.

forest

android free

ios php 99.00


this app would plant one tree in your forest everytime you finish a task. although, if you exit, the plant will die. forest is great for preventing distractions like social media because of that feature.

study bunny

android free

ios free


this app is quite similar to forest but you'll be taking care of a bunny instead of a forest. along with a cute bunny, you'll be able to make flashcards and to-do lists in this app. it's also a great free alternative to the forest app for ios users.
