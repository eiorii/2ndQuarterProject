How do you manage your time?

This is a survey, or a test, on how you manage time and under pressure. The test has 12 questions about how you manage your time.























Almost Never Often Almost Always
1. Do you have a tendency to multitask?
2. Do you get caught up with the details when working on a project?
3. Do you think you can work better under pressure?
4. Would you rather do work immediately with no plan at all?
5. Do you have a hard time transitioning from one task to the other?
6. Do you get bored easily when doing repetitive tasks?
7. Do you always procrastinate on your projects?
8. Do you believe you can do work better alone than with groupmates?
9. Do you particularly hate the small details in your projects?
10. Do you always get distracted when doing work?
11. Do you have high personal standards on your work?
12. Do you rather think of the big, lofty, essential parts
of the project than the smaller yet crucial components?
